# StudyGenie AI 🧞‍♂️ + LevelUp AI 🚀

Welcome to **StudyGenie AI**, an intelligent, gamified learning platform designed to transform how you study. By leveraging advanced AI, StudyGenie turns static study materials into interactive, personalized, and highly effective educational experiences.

## ⚠️ The Problem It Solves

Traditional studying is often passive and inefficient. Students spend hours reading PDFs, textbooks, or notes, which leads to poor knowledge retention. Key challenges include:
* **Passive Consumption:** Reading without testing leads to the "illusion of competence."
* **Lack of Personalization:** Standard materials don't adapt to a student's individual weak points.
* **High Cost of Tutoring:** 1-on-1 personalized tutoring is expensive and not accessible 24/7.
* **Burnout & Low Motivation:** Studying can feel like a chore without immediate feedback or a sense of progression.

## 💡 The Solution It Provides

StudyGenie AI tackles these problems by applying cognitive science principles (like active recall and spaced repetition) wrapped in a highly engaging, gamified interface. 

It provides the following solutions:
1. **Active Recall via AI Quizzes:** Instead of just reading, the app generates dynamic quizzes based on your uploaded materials to actively test your memory.
2. **24/7 Personalized AI Tutor:** Stuck on a concept? The built-in AI Tutor understands the context of your uploaded documents and can explain complex topics simply, provide real-life examples, or test you on the spot.
3. **Targeted Learning:** The app tracks your accuracy and retention, identifying "Weak Concepts" so you know exactly where to focus your efforts.
4. **Gamified Motivation:** By introducing XP, levels, daily streaks, and achievement badges, StudyGenie makes learning addictive and rewarding.

## ✨ Key Features

* 📚 **Learning Hub:** Upload study materials (text or PDFs) and let the AI extract core concepts and summaries.
* 🧠 **Context-Aware AI Tutor:** A chat interface powered by Google's Gemini AI that answers questions specifically based on the materials you are studying.
* ⚡ **Dynamic Quizzes:** Auto-generated multiple-choice questions with difficulty scaling (Easy, Medium, Hard, Boss).
* 📈 **Progress & Analytics:** Visual growth maps, mastery mix charts, and skill tracking (Speed, Accuracy, Retention, Consistency) using Recharts.
* 🎮 **Gamification System:** Earn XP for completing quizzes, level up your "Scholar" rank, maintain daily streaks, and unlock badges.
* 🌙 **Dark Mode:** Fully supported dark mode for comfortable late-night study sessions.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **Frontend:** React 19, TypeScript, Vite
* **Styling:** Tailwind CSS
* **Animations:** Framer Motion
* **Data Visualization:** Recharts
* **Icons:** Lucide React
* **AI Integration:** `@google/genai` (Gemini API)

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. Upload a study material in the **Study** tab.
2. Take a **Quiz** to establish your baseline knowledge.
3. Check your **Stats** to see your weak points.
4. Chat with the **AI Tutor** to clarify concepts you missed.
5. Watch your XP grow and level up!
